Aura Geode: Hyperbaric Therapy & Digital Twin System

A therapeutic-grade hyperbaric system designed for two functions: to deliver targeted oxygen protocols and to serve as a high-fidelity biometric data-gathering platform for the creation of personal digital twins.

Two Models, One Protocol

Mass Production Model

Engineered for mass production and accessibility, the steel-frame unit is the foundation of the cooperative network. It features a spacious interior with both standing and seating room and a conventional walk-in door. This design prioritizes modularity and cost-efficiency, enabling deployment in clinics, wellness centers, and community hubs.

Mythopoetic Model

The Crystalline Geode is an ovoid-shaped capsule constructed from a pressure-rated geopolymer composite infused with 99% pure crystalline quartz sand from Minjerribah. Access is facilitated by the 'Egg Eats the Throne' mechanism: the user is seated on an external recliner which is then drawn into the capsule before the shell seals. This model is designed for a psychologically immersive, seating-only experience.

The 120-Hour Aura Genesis Protocol

The core user experience is a structured 60-session, 120-hour journey designed to create a comprehensive digital twin. The protocol is structured in three distinct phases.

1

Discovery (Sessions 1-20)

This initial phase maps the user's unique physiological and psychological dose-response to a wide range of controlled stimuli, such as different atmospheric compositions, light frequencies, and soundscapes.

2

AI-Design (Sessions 21-40)

The system's AI Core acts as a composer, designing complete, synergistic 2-hour therapeutic sessions. It sequences different modalities to achieve specific outcomes like deep relaxation or enhanced cognitive focus, creating a protocol uniquely tailored to the individual.

3

Validation (Sessions 41-60)

The AI refines the user's optimal 'Prime Protocol' and runs placebo-controlled versions (e.g., delivering normal air when the user expects pure oxygen) to rigorously quantify the user's individual placebo response and validate the protocol's effects.

Integrated Biometric Sensing & Health Outcomes

Real-Time Data Capture

Each session captures a high-fidelity stream of biometric data through a suite of integrated and wearable sensors:

  • EEG headset to monitor brainwave activity.
  • PPG/ECG wristband to track Heart Rate Variability (HRV), heart rate, and Galvanic Skin Response (GSR).
  • SpO2 clip to measure blood oxygen saturation.
  • Integrated sensors for seat posture pressure, plus microphones and cameras for capturing subjective feedback.

Tracking the Biological Reset

The protocol is designed to achieve a quantifiable biological reset. At key milestones, an optional 'Aura Bio-Kit' allows for the analysis of biological samples (saliva, urine, breath, micro-blood) to track changes in:

  • Epigenetic clocks and telomere length.
  • Systemic inflammatory markers.
  • Hormonal balance and optimization.
  • Mitochondrial efficiency and cardiovascular fitness.
