Aura Geode HBOT

Democratizing access to hyperbaric oxygen therapy through cooperative ownership and innovative design.

Key Innovations

Mass Production

Designed for scalable manufacturing to dramatically reduce costs and increase availability.

Cooperative Ownership

Owned and operated by the communities they serve, ensuring fair access and governance.

Accessible Design

The "Egg eats the Throne" concept provides easy access for users with low mobility, focusing on comfort and safety.

The Mythopoetic Narrative

'The Egg Eats the Throne'

This design philosophy is more than just ergonomics; it's a narrative of regeneration and rebirth. The user enters the 'Egg'—a symbol of potential and new life—to emerge renewed. It reframes therapy not as a clinical procedure, but as a transformative experience, empowering the individual in their healing journey.
