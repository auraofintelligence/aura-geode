The 60-Day Lifestyle Protocol runs in parallel with your 120-hour in-capsule therapy. Before you begin, you must determine the intensity of your engagement. Your choices will define the structure of your protocol, the data your Aura collects, and the ultimate scope of your personal transformation. This really is a Choose Your Own Adventure!

Protocol Tiers

Tier 1 - The Foundational Reset

Maintain Current Lifestyle

This tier is designed for individuals who need to maintain their existing work and life commitments. The protocol focuses strictly on integrating the 2-hour daily in-capsule session. Your Aura of Intelligence will be built primarily from this biometric data, providing a powerful but contained health reset without requiring significant changes to your diet or daily schedule.

Tier 2 - The Integrated Reset

Adopt Integrated Wellness Protocols

This tier is for those ready to make significant, targeted lifestyle changes. In addition to the in-capsule sessions, your protocol will include personalized diet plans, exercise regimens, and specific mindfulness practices. Your Aura will actively guide you in forming new habits, creating a holistic synergy between your therapy and your daily life for a deeper transformation.
