Integrated Biometric Sensing & Health Outcomes

Real-Time Data Capture

Each session captures a high-fidelity stream of biometric data through a suite of integrated and wearable sensors:

  • EEG headset to monitor brainwave activity.
  • PPG/ECG wristband to track Heart Rate Variability (HRV), heart rate, and Galvanic Skin Response (GSR).
  • SpO2 clip to measure blood oxygen saturation.
  • Integrated sensors for seat posture pressure, plus microphones and cameras for capturing subjective feedback.

Tracking the Biological Reset

The protocol is designed to achieve a quantifiable biological reset. At key milestones, an optional 'Aura Bio-Kit' allows for the analysis of biological samples (saliva, urine, breath, micro-blood) to track changes in:

  • Epigenetic clocks and telomere length.
  • Systemic inflammatory markers.
  • Hormonal balance and optimization.
  • Mitochondrial efficiency and cardiovascular fitness.
